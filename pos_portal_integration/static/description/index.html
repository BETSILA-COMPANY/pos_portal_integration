POS Customer Portal Orders

This app enables customers to order products that are available in the Point of Sale (POS) system directly through their portal accounts. Once logged in, customers can browse products, place orders, and view a history of all their previous orders through a new section called "My POS Orders". These orders are automatically recorded and visible to POS managers as drafts, allowing for easy processing through an active POS session.
