POS Portal Integration for Customer Orders

Empower Your Customers with Self-Service Point of Sale Ordering

This module revolutionizes your Odoo Point of Sale experience by allowing your registered customers to create POS orders directly from their personalized portal account. Seamlessly integrated with your existing Odoo setup, it enhances customer convenience, reduces manual order entry for your staff, and provides a streamlined ordering process accessible 24/7.

Key Features

  • Customer-Friendly Order Interface: Intuitive portal page for easy product selection and quantity adjustment.
  • Real-time Product List: Customers see products marked "Available in PoS" from your catalog.
  • Automatic Pricelist & Tax Application: Orders respect customer-specific pricelists and applicable taxes (based on your Odoo configuration).
  • Direct POS Order Creation: Submitted orders are automatically created as Draft POS Orders in your Odoo backend.
  • Order History in Portal: Customers can view their past POS orders directly from their portal account.
  • Seamless User Experience: Fully integrated with Odoo's native portal design.

Benefits

  • **Enhance Customer Satisfaction:** Provide 24/7 self-service convenience.
  • **Increase Efficiency:** Reduce staff workload for order taking and data entry.
  • **Streamline Operations:** Automate the creation of POS orders.
  • **Improve Accuracy:** Minimize errors from manual order transcription.

Usage

  1. Install the 'POS Portal Integration' module.
  2. Ensure your POS configuration is set up and there is an open POS session.
  3. Ensure products intended for portal ordering have 'Available in PoS' checked.
  4. Customers will find a new 'POS Orders' section in their portal dashboard.
  5. They can click 'New POS Order', select products and quantities, and submit.
  6. The order will appear in your Odoo backend under Point of Sale > Orders.
